# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22/02/2025

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to joaoestima.com. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
We collect information you directly provide when:
- Submitting contact forms (name, email, phone number, and message content)
- Creating an account (name and email address)

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
We automatically collect:
- IP addresses
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- Usage data through Google Analytics
- Cookies and similar tracking technologies

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Respond to your inquiries and contact requests
- Provide and maintain our services
- Analyze website usage to improve user experience
- Send confirmation emails for contact form submissions
- Protect against fraudulent or unauthorized activity

## 4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Your data is stored securely and only accessible to authorized personnel.

## 5. Third-Party Services

### 5.1 Google Analytics
We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic. Google Analytics may collect:
- Pages visited
- Time spent on site
- Traffic sources
- Device information

For more information, please review [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data

- Request deletion of your data

- Request data portability
